Really Cool Placeholders

A quick and simple service for "getting images" to use as placeholders in your designs or code.

Just put your image size (height & width) after the URL and you'll get a nice-looking random placeholder image.

Need an image? Try this: <img src="http://rainier.apphb.com/270/400" height="270" width="400"/>

Something less random? Use a keyword (like beach): <img src="http://rainier.apphb.com/beach/270/400" height="270" width="400"/>

Inspired by placekitten.com (which was inspired by placehold.it). Yeah, I know. Original.
